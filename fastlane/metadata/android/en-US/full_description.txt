Betabase pulls upcoming sport climbing competitions from multiple sources into one fast, filterable list.

<b>Sources</b>
• IFSC World Cups, World Championships and Continental Cups (live calendar)
• NKBV — Dutch national and regional series
• Sport Climbing Australia — national cups, championships and para events

<b>Filter how you watch</b>
• Region — World, Netherlands, Australia
• Discipline — Boulder, Lead, Speed, Combined
• Round — Qualifier, Semi, Final
• Audience — Women, Men, Youth

<b>Designed for climbers</b>
• Bold, flat colour-coded cards: orange for Boulder, blue for Lead, yellow for Speed, purple for Combined
• Tap any event card to jump to the source's official page — IFSC events open the livestream, NKBV and SCA events open the registration / detail page
• Pull-to-refresh to fetch the latest IFSC schedule
• Events grouped by day with bold date headers

<b>What's coming next</b>
More national federations and continental councils, smarter notifications for finals you care about, and offline-first caching.

Betabase is open source: https://github.com/SimonSchubert/Betabase
